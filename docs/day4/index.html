Japan has diverse climate

  • Japan has a long shape from North to South 日本は南北に長い

  • Variation in climate: subarctic 亜寒帯to subtropical 亜熱帯

  • Variation in elevation

  • Highly diverse ecosystems

Japan has complicated geography

  • Situated at boundaries of multiple tectonic plates 複数のプレートが一緒になるところに位置する

  • Extremely high geologic activity (earthquakes and mountain formation) 地震や火山が多い

https://www.researchgate.net/profile/W-Spakman/publication/353594344

Japan has complicated geography

  • Separated from mainland ca. 25 million years ago やく2,500万年前に大陸から離れた

  • Re-connected at three points　３カ所で再度に大陸と繋がった

    • Hokkadio to Russia
    • Honshu to Korea
    • Okinawa to Taiwan

Japan is affected by glaciers

  • Glaciers grow and shrink due to changes in global temperature 氷河は地球の温度に伴い、大きくなったり縮んだりする

https://www.snexplores.org/article/scientists-say-glacier

Glaciers come and go in cycles

  • When glaciers are large (glacial maximum 氷期), sea levels are low

  • When glaciers are small (inter-glacial periods 間氷期), sea levels are high

  • Glacial maxima and inter-glacial periods repeat in a cycle　氷期と間氷期が繰り返される

  • It is currently an inter-glacial period　現在は間氷期

Connections to the continent shaped biodiversity in Japan

  • Organisms moved into Japan from the mainland when it was connected (glacial maxima) 氷期の間に日本は大陸と繋がっていたので、生物たちが日本へ渡ることができた

  • They became isolated and evolved into new species 日本で隔離されるようになって、新しい種へと進化した

Deep-sea troughs shape biodiversity

  • The water is very deep in the Tsugaru strait between Hokkaido and Honshu, so those did not become connected during the LGM 津軽海峡がとても深いので、最終氷期極大期の間でも北海道と本州がつながることはなかった。

Modified from https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/d/d9/Japan_glaciation.gif

Deep-sea troughs shape biodiversity

  • Animals that cannot easily cross over water could not disperse from Hokkaido into Honshu 水を簡単に渡れない動物は、北海道から本州に入ることが出来なかった。

  • There is a major difference in species between Hokkaido and Honshu today 現在、北海道と本州の間には大きな種の違いがある。

  • This border is known as “Blakiston’s line” この境界線は “ブラキストン線”と呼ばれている

Summary of key concepts

  • Japan has diverse climate, which means many species adapted for different climates live in a small area 日本には多様な気候があり、異なる気候に適応した多くの種が狭い地域に生息している。

  • Japan has complicated geological history, which means many different species evolved over time 日本には複雑な地質学的歴史があり、そのために様々な種が進化することが出来た。

  • Growth of glaciers affects sea level, and thereby connections between islands 氷河の成長は海面に影響を与え、それによって島と島のつながりが変化する。
